## Strategy Overview

The Momentum Scalper strategy aims to capture fast impulse moves during intraday trends by requiring:
1. **EMA Stack:** EMA9 > EMA21 > EMA50 (bullish) or inverse (bearish)
2. **Swing Breakout:** Close breaks above/below recent swing high/low
3. **Volume Confirmation:** Volume > 1.5× SMA20
4. **Risk Management:** ATR-based stop loss (1.0-1.5x) with 2R take profit

## Key Findings

### 1. Consistent Win Rate Problem
All versions show a **~33-34% win rate** - this is the core issue. The strategy loses 2 out of 3 trades, which is difficult to overcome even with a 2:1 reward/risk ratio.

### 2. Exit Analysis
| Exit Reason | Trades | Win Rate | P&L Impact |
|-------------|--------|----------|------------|
| **Stop Loss** | ~65% of trades | 0% | Large losses |
| **Take Profit** | ~35% of trades | 99%+ | Gains don't offset losses |

The problem: When the strategy hits a stop, it loses the full 1R. When it hits TP, it gains 2R. But with only 33% win rate:
- Expected value: (0.33 × 2R) - (0.67 × 1R) = 0.66R - 0.67R = **-0.01R per trade**
- This is essentially breakeven before fees, negative after fees

### 3. Why It Doesn't Work on BTC

**Late Entry Problem:**
- By the time EMA9 > EMA21 > EMA50 aligns AND price breaks the swing high, the move has often already happened
- The strategy is buying highs and selling lows in choppy conditions

**False Breakouts:**
- BTC frequently has volume spikes that don't lead to sustained moves
- The 1.5-2.0x volume threshold isn't selective enough

**EMA Stack Lag:**
- EMA50 on 5M is a 4+ hour lookback - by the time all three align, the trend may be exhausted

### 4. Version Comparisons

**V1 (Baseline):** Too many trades, excessive drawdown
**V2 (Quality filters):** Reduced trades by 89%, better drawdown control, still unprofitable
**V3 (Trend filter):** Made things worse - more trades, deeper losses

## Conclusion

**The Momentum Scalper strategy, as currently configured, is NOT profitable on BTC over the tested 3-year period.**

### Why It Fails:
1. Entry timing is too late - catches moves after they've occurred
2. Win rate (~33%) is too low for the 2R reward/risk ratio
3. Stop losses are hit frequently due to BTC's volatility and chop
4. The EMA stack + breakout combination creates a lagging indicator

### Potential Improvements (Not Tested):
1. **Earlier entry:** Consider entering on EMA cross before full stack alignment
2. **Different timeframe:** Try 15M or 1H candles to reduce noise
3. **Market regime filter:** Only trade during high volatility periods
4. **Reduce leverage:** 3x amplifies losses; 1-2x may improve survival
5. **Dynamic position sizing:** Reduce size during drawdowns
6. **Add confirmation:** Require RSI or MACD confirmation

### Recommendation:
**Do not deploy this strategy live** without significant modifications. The consistent negative expectancy across all tested variations suggests the core logic needs rethinking, not just parameter tuning.
